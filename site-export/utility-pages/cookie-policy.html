This Cookie Policy explains how Lumos (“we,” “us,” “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on our website and services (the “Service”).

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, improve performance, and understand usage.

We may also use similar technologies like pixels, local storage, and tags for similar purposes.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential cookies
Required for core functionality (e.g., security, page navigation). The Service may not work properly without these.

Performance & analytics cookies
Help us understand how visitors use the Service (e.g., pages visited, time spent) so we can improve.

Functional cookies
Remember your preferences (e.g., language, region) to provide a smoother experience.

Marketing cookies (optional)
Used to measure campaign performance or show relevant content. (Only include this if you actually run marketing tracking.)

3. How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Keep the Service secure and functioning.
  • Save user preferences.
  • Measure and improve performance and reliability.
  • Understand which content is most useful.

4. Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Block or delete cookies.
  • Set your browser to notify you when cookies are being used.

Note: Disabling cookies may impact the functionality of the Service.

5. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies may be set by third-party services we use (e.g., analytics providers, embedded content). Those providers may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date above.
